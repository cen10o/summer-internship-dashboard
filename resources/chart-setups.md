# Survey Data Transformation

## Intern Engagement Chart(s)

### Levels of Comfort Asking for Help & Inclusion (Pre & Post)

**Survey Questions to Measure & Respective Labels:**

- **Mentor Support Access:** "I feel comfortable asking my mentor/supervisor for help."
- **Program Support Access:** "I feel comfortable reaching out to the Next Gen Career Pathways Program for help."
- **Sense of Belonging:** "I already feel a sense of belonging at Valley Water." (pre), "I felt a strong sense of belonging at Valley Water." (post)

**How the chart would look:**
A chart where grey bars represent pre measures and blue bars represent post measures for each question. The x-axis is each question label and the y-axis represents the average rating of agreement from 1–5.

### Friday Learning Content Delivery

**Survey Questions to Measure & Respective Labels:**

- **Presenter Quality:** "The presenter(s) was/were knowledgeable and engaging."
- **Effectiveness of Format:** "The format was effective."
- **Professional Development Relevance:** "The session content was relevant to my professional development."
- **Obtained New Knowledge/Skills:** "I gained new knowledge or skills I can apply."
- **Overall Satisfaction:** "How satisfied are you overall with this Learning Friday session?"

**How the chart would look:**
A line chart where presenter quality, effectiveness of format, professional development relevance, obtained new knowledge/skills, and overall satisfaction are plotted as separate lines across each Friday Learning Event. The x-axis represents each event and the y-axis represents the average rating from a scale of 1–5.

### Summer Intern Fun Bus Feedback

**Survey Questions to Measure & Respective Labels:**

- **Overall Satisfaction:** "How satisfied are you overall with the summer intern fun bus?"
- **Connection/Belonging:** "The summer intern fun bus helped me feel more connected to Valley Water and its mission."

**How the chart will look:**
A bar chart where the x-axis represents the labels and the y-axis represents the agreement rating from 1–5.

## Mentor Experience Chart(s)

### Mentor Satisfaction & Program Support (Mentor Survey)

**Survey Questions to Measure & Respective Labels:**

**Would Return as Mentor:** "I would mentor an intern in this program again in the future."

**Resources & Support** (average of the 3 questions):

- "I had the necessary resources and support to mentor effectively."
- "The program provided helpful materials or tools (e.g., mentor handbook, resources for mentors Aqua page)."
- "Next Gen Career Pathways Program staff were available and responsive when I had questions or concerns."

**Felt Prepared for Role** (average of the 3 questions):

- "I felt confident and prepared to mentor my intern."
- "I received adequate training or orientation for my role as a mentor."
- "The training and/or materials provided were helpful in preparing me for mentoring."

**Satisfied with Mentoring Experience** (average of the 3 questions):

- "I am satisfied with my overall experience as a mentor in the Next Gen Career Pathways Program."
- "I found the experience of mentoring rewarding."
- "I found that mentoring an intern contributed to my own professional growth (e.g., coaching, leadership, communication skills)."

**How it would look:**
Single Combined Bar Chart showing all four subsections:

- **X-axis:** Each subsection label (Would Return, Resources & Support, Felt Prepared, Satisfied with Experience)
- **Y-axis:** Average rating from 1–5
